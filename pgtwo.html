Data visualisations circulating on social media can mislead.

They can give the impression that there is only one way to interpret the facts, especially concerning sensitive topics.

However, the truth is that data visualisations are not always value-neutral or independent of biases. They represent one of many possible interpretations of reality.

Misleading data visualisations can be harmful, threatening our freedom and our right to accurate information.

Being critical is essential.

With this Field Guide, you can explore and recognise the threats posed by misleading data visualisations circulating on social media during social crises.
